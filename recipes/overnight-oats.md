# Overnight Oats

A healthy make-ahead breakfast.

Tags: breakfast, healthy, meal-prep
Serves: 1
Calories: 350 | Protein: 12g

## Ingredients

- 50g rolled oats
- 150ml milk
- 100g yoghurt
- 1 tbsp honey
- Fruit, to serve

## Method

1. Combine oats, milk, yoghurt, and honey in a jar.

2. Refrigerate overnight.

3. Top with fruit and serve.
